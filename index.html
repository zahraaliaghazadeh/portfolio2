About Me

My name is Zahra Ali Aghazadeh and I go by "Yalda"; a persian name. I have had little experience with coding in NODET highschool with robotics classes. I then studied Electrical Engineering for two years in Iran. After moving to Seattle in 2014, I started taking pre-med courses at Bellevue College to follow my newfound passion and worked over fulltime between two jobs as a pharmacy technician and a math tutor. I then graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Biochemistry from University of Washington Seattle in 2018. Unfortunately, I did not get accepted to UW medical school and the jobs available for a Biochemistry undergrad did not interest me. I had spent too much effort trying to make something that was not a hundred percent what I wanted, work.
It was a "Point of No Return". It was time to change my destination.
I am looking forward to the opportunities that I may have after completing my bootcamp in May of 2020. I am hoping to be able to expand my knowledge furthur by getting a master's degree in computer science. I love volleyball, hiking and traveling. At some point in the future, it would be a great advantage to work remotely and travel.
